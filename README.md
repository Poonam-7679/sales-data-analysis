
# 📊 Sales Data Analysis - Business Intelligence Case Study

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project analyzes sales data to identify growth opportunities and improve profitability.

### Key Results:
- 💰 **Total Revenue:** $8,900
- 📈 **Total Profit:** $2,162 (approx. 24% margin)
- 🏆 **Top Product:** Product A
- 🌍 **Best Region:** North (~40% of sales)

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Product performance comparison
- Regional sales distribution
- Category-wise profitability
- Actionable business recommendations

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales show upward trend from January to March
- Product A generates highest revenue
- North region contributes largest sales share
- Electronics drives majority of revenue

## 💡 Recommendations
- Increase Electronics and Clothing inventory
- Focus marketing on North and South regions
- Review Grocery category pricing strategy

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn)
- Jupyter Notebook
- GitHub
